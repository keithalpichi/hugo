My Timeline

Below is a timeline from when I first started programming to today. I decided to create this to show you the path I took, why I took it, the assumptions and questions I had, the challenges I faced and how I overcame them.

The reason why I wanted to create a timeline is to advise and set a path for you so that you can learn to program effectively and efficiently. When I first started I didn't know where to start and I had my assumptions about learning software programming.

Since I didn't have prior experience or education in computer science I didn't get the guidance or connections I would have received if I was in school. Going back to school wasn't an ideal option for me and it may not be for you as well.

Why Follow My Timeline?

So I hope this timeline can help you in the following ways:

  • Give you guidance to where to start and clear up confusion and assumptions about software programming
  • Provide you with different options based on your interests
  • Help you avoid mistakes, wasted time, frustration and confusion
  • Help you face and overcome challenges because you WILL face them
  • Make you become an effective and efficient programmer
  • Point you to the appropriate resources and experts to learn software enginnering

At the bottom of this page is the link to my guide on how you should learn based on my personal experience. Before going there I suggest you read this page first.
