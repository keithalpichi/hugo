Career and Interview Preparation

If you want to achieve a career in software programming you need to prove you have the skills and experience to code. As a self-taught programmer without formal education you must show employers your projects and contributions to the industry. This, along with attending conferences and meetups, is most effective way for a self-taught programmer to gain exposure and ultimately get an interview.

When you have built a few personal projects and have contributed to open-source projects the next steps is to prepare for a future interview. The most popular resources for interview preparation are:

Resource Description Free?
Introduction to Algorithms Go-to resource for solidying your knowledge of algorithms No
Cracking the Coding Interview Go-to resource to help a software engineer perform well before, during and after a technical interview. No
