September 2015- Ruby

From what I read online the community is huge and it's widely used with the Rails web framework to build big web applications. I considered Ruby on Rails because of this and there was a local Ruby on Rails developer bootcamp that I was interested in.

Reflection Comments
Why I pursued this path I am not too sure why I first pursued Ruby but I believe it was because of all the suggestions I got
What I thought I was struggling to grasp the fundamentals of programming- data types, data structures, loops, control flow, etc.
What I learned from this experience I had to focus not on a language but the basics of computer science and object-oriented programming (OOP).
Advice Choose any language but focus on the fundamentals of computer science and OOP. Consider taking an introductory computer science course or visit the resource page to watch the free Youtube course of MIT's Introduction to Computer Science and Programming.
